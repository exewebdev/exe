About Us

We are the Computer Science Club at Texas State University. Our purpose is to aid Computer Science students to further their skills, network with like-minded individuals, and enjoy the company of a diverse student body gathered around a shared interest in computing and all of its related fields. The EXE Computer Science Club is organized to offer semester projects, group meetings, and a general atmosphere of camaraderie for students of any major to present and discuss topics of all kind.
